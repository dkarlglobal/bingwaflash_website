02

What Bingwa Flash Does

Bingwa Flash is an Android automation tool designed exclusively for Safaricom agents in Kenya. The App reads incoming M-Pesa payment SMS messages and automatically executes the corresponding USSD sequences on the agent's device, enabling faster fulfilment of data bundles, airtime, and other offers.

Additional features available within the Service include:

  • Multi-step and fallback USSD automation
  • SMS relay between sender and receiver agent devices
  • A personal web order page accessible at bingwaflash.co.ke/order/?u=username
  • STK push payment processing for web orders via M-Pesa
  • Client registration and management (clientele list)
  • Transaction scheduling and retry logic
  • Agent alerts, blacklisting, and transaction logs

Bingwa Flash does not initiate any payment or USSD action without a corresponding M-Pesa trigger SMS or explicit agent configuration. The App only acts on payments that have already been made.

Eligibility

To use Bingwa Flash as an agent you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Hold a valid and active Safaricom agent account or till number

End customers who use an agent's web order page to place orders do not need a Bingwa Flash account, but must provide a valid Safaricom M-Pesa number to complete a transaction.

Web Orders & Payments

Agents with web orders enabled receive a personal order page where customers can purchase offers by paying via M-Pesa STK push. By placing a web order you agree that:

  • Payment is processed directly through Safaricom M-Pesa — Bingwa Flash does not store or handle your M-Pesa credentials
  • The STK push prompt expires after 70 seconds — if you do not enter your PIN within this window, no charge is made
  • Once payment is confirmed by M-Pesa, the agent's app will process your order automatically
  • Fulfilment depends on the agent's device being online and the Safaricom USSD service being available at the time of payment
  • The offer price displayed on the order page is set by the agent and is the amount you will be charged

Bingwa Flash is a fulfilment automation tool. All payment transactions are governed by Safaricom M-Pesa's own terms. In case of a payment dispute, contact the agent directly or reach Safaricom on *234#.

Refund and reversal requests for failed orders must be raised directly with the agent. Bingwa Flash is not a party to the payment transaction between the customer and the agent.
