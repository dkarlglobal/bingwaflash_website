01 — Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to Bingwa Flash ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website at bingwaflash.co.ke.

By downloading or using the Bingwa Flash app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

02 — Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you register or use the App:

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number for account verification
  • Usage data and in-app interactions
  • Support messages and communications

We also automatically collect certain technical data:

  • App version and crash reports
03 — Data Use

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve the App:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Send email verification during registration
  • Deliver automation alerts and SMS relay features
  • Respond to support requests promptly
  • Detect and prevent security issues or abuse
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the App
  • Send important product update notices
04 — Email

Email Communications

When you register, we send a verification link to your email from noreply@bingwa-flash.firebaseapp.com via Firebase Authentication. This email is transactional and required to complete your registration.

We do not send unsolicited marketing or promotional emails. All communications are strictly account-related and transactional.

05 — Sharing

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. We may only share your data with:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and verification services
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law
06 — Retention

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Verification links expire within 24 hours. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at any time.

07 — Security

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit and secure infrastructure provided by Firebase/Google Cloud.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security against all threats.

08 — Children

Children's Privacy

Bingwa Flash is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

09 — Your Rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Request a portable copy of your data

Contact us at support@bingwaflash.co.ke. We will respond within 14 business days.

10 — Changes

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
