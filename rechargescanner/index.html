Process

Three steps.
Zero headaches.

Choose your mode

Use single scan, bulk scan, focus box, full screen scanner, or manual entry — whatever fits your situation.

Confirm the digits

Scanning pauses and shows a dialog where you can edit, save, cancel, or run — no surprise charges.

Run USSD

Recharge through your selected SIM and keep the network response in your voucher history automatically.

Privacy-first by design

Camera is used only to detect voucher digits. Recharge actions require your explicit confirmation. No data is shared without your knowledge.
