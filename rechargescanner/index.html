SIM and carrier codes

How it works

1. Choose your mode

Use single scan, bulk scan, focus box, full screen scanner, or manual entry.

2. Confirm the digits

The app pauses scanning logic and shows a dialog where you can edit, save, cancel, or run.

3. Run USSD

Recharge through the selected SIM and keep the network response in your voucher history.

Privacy-first utility
