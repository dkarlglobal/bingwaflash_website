Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A focused set of tools built specifically for Safaricom agents who need reliability and speed above all else.

USSD Automation
Intercepts incoming M-Pesa SMS and dials USSD automatically — no screen unlock, no manual input, 24/7.
Multi-Step USSD
Full multi-step flows with per-step responses, conditional navigation, and stop-when-changed detection.
Fallback USSD
Automatically switches to a backup USSD code when the primary flow fails. Zero manual intervention.
SMS Relay
Forward M-Pesa messages to partner agents. Sender and receiver relay modes run simultaneously.
Web Orders
Your own order page at bingwaflash.co.ke/yourname. Customers pay via STK push, app fulfils instantly.
Offers Bot
SMS chatbot that recommends offers, answers customer inquiries, and collects the recipient number.
Scheduler
Schedule transactions for a future time with configurable retry windows on failure.
Agent Alerts
Real-time SMS alerts for automation-off events, failures, reversals, and blacklisted numbers.
Dual SIM
Assign offers to specific SIM slots. Run different services on different SIMs simultaneously.
Transaction Logs
Full searchable history — status, USSD response, failure reason, original SMS. Filter by date.
Blacklist
Block specific customers, disable offers individually, prevent duplicate executions automatically.
Auto-Save Contacts
Saves paying customers with a configurable suffix. Handles duplicates intelligently.

How It Works

Four steps.
Fully automatic.

01
Customer Pays
M-Pesa payment arrives on your till or number. App reads the SMS instantly — no delays.
02
Offer Matched
Amount and payment mode matched against your configured offers. Correct USSD selected.
03
USSD Dialled
Accessibility service executes the USSD sequence silently. Screen stays off. No interaction needed.
04
Done & Logged
Result saved with full USSD response. Automatic retry on failure — or flagged for manual review.
