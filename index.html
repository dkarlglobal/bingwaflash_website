The Flow
How It Works
01
Customer Pays
Customer sends M-Pesa to your till or number. App intercepts the SMS in real time — no polling, no delay.
02
Offer Matched
Amount and payment mode matched against your configured offers. Right USSD template is selected automatically.
03
USSD Executed
Accessibility service dials the USSD sequence silently. No screen unlock required. Screen can be off.
04
Logged & Done
Result logged with full USSD response. On failure, app retries automatically or flags for manual review.
